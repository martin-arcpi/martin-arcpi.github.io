Village History

The village first appears in the historial record in the year 1576 under the name "Salih".

Village Geography

The Dabene village is located 5 kilometers away from the city of Karlovo which is the birthplace of Vasil Levski. The 2024 census shows that the population of the village is 1454. The Stryama river is located to the south of the village. The village is famous for the Dabene Treasure found in 2004 which is displayed in the Natural History Museum.

Village Economy

Predominantly the population participates in farming and agriculture while younger people work for the army in the city of Karlovo. The nearby peak of "Konnik" attracts hand glinding ethusiasts from all over the world.

Notable People

  • Teacher Lilo Nikolov - The founder of the only school in the village. The school was closed in 2008 due to lack of students.
  • Nikolai Buhalov - An olympic gold medalist specialising in canoeing. He won 2 gold medals at 1992 Barcelona olympics. He is the only bulgarian to have won two gold medals in a single olympic games.
